My Ballet Journey

From my first ballet class at age five to performing on stages across the country, ballet has shaped who I am. I believe every dancer has a unique story to tell through movement, and I'm here to help you discover yours.

My Philosophy

Dance is not just about perfect technique – it's about expressing your soul through every gesture. I teach with patience, encouragement, and a deep respect for the art form.

Gallery

Our beautiful studio features floor-to-ceiling mirrors, professional ballet barres, and sprung floors designed to protect dancers' joints. The space is filled with natural light, creating an inspiring atmosphere for learning.








Students of all ages are welcome, from tiny dancers taking their first steps to adults rediscovering their passion. Every class begins with a warm welcome and ends with accomplishment.







Essential Beginner Positions

Position Foot Placement Arm Position
First Position Heels together, toes turned outward forming a straight line Arms rounded, held low in front of the body
Second Position Feet apart, heels aligned with shoulders Arms extended to the sides at shoulder height
Third Position One heel against the arch of the other foot One arm rounded low, one extended to side
